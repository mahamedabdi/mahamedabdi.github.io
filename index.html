Fun fact about me:

Every Sunday morning for the month of April to September I coach a youth basketball league. I’ve been doing this for the past 5 years and it’s helped me in ways I couldn’t even imagine. It’s helped me first and foremost work on my patience with others, I learned that people pick things up at different speeds and not everyone is the same. In addition to that I learned how to become a better communicator, some of the kids don’t speak English well as English isn’t their first language so I had to adapt and learn how to communicate them in an efficient and concise way so they do not feel left out. Lastly I learned how to be a leader, me being the coach I was the one they looked up to. If I wasn’t on time they wouldn’t be in time so I had to lead by example so I could show them the way to act on and off the court. Coaching has honestly made me a better person and il continue to do it as long as I can.
